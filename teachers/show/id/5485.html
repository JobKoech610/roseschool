Francesco Graziotti

Prof. DENIS LEBOEUF

Professor of Civil Engineering

Department of Civil Engineering - Faculty of Sciences and Engineering
Laval University - Quebec City, Canada

EDUCATION

- GRADUATE and UNDERGRADUATE TEACHING IN CIVIL ENGINEERING:

Undergraduate: Soil Mechanics, Foundation Engineering, Foundation Engineering Design;

Graduate: Geotechnical Earthquake Engineering since 2000

- RESEARCH IN GEOTECHNICAL EARTHQUAKE ENGINEERING

Sponsored scientific research (since 1994) from Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC) in geotechnical earthquake engineering

ACADEMIC EMPLOYMENT

- Professor of Civil Engineering, Department of Civil Engineering, Faculty of Sciences and Engineering, Université Laval, Quebec City, Canada; Specialization in Geotechnical Earthquake Engineering

- Active geotechnical research and consulting collaboration with all the main engineering and governemental agencies in Québec: Ministère des transports du Québec, Hydro-Québec, École Polytechnique, Ministry of Health, Ministry of Education, IRSST, AECOM, City of Québec, Protection Civile Canada, SNC-Lavalin.

- Sept 2013-Feb. 2014 and May-June 2016 : Invited Researcher, Université Paris-Est, IFSTTAR, GERS (Géotechnique, Environnement, Risques naturels et Sciences de la terre),  Laboratoire Séismes et Vibrations (with Dr. J-F Semblat), Paris, France

- May 2012-present : Member, Centre d'Études Interuniversitaire des Structures sous Charges Extrêmes (CEISCE), University of de Sherbrooke (PI : Pr. P. Paultre)

- Fellow, Engineering Institute of Canada (2013)
