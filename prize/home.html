Prize Selection Committee
Honouring individuals for creativity, innovation, research and professional achievements and excellence in education in the field of earthquake engineering and engineering seismology.

Nominations for the Nigel Priestley International Prize 2016 are now open

We encourage individuals in government, private firms, academia, and the international community to nominate eligible candidates no later than January 31st 2016 to prize.nomination@roseschool.it.

For a nomination the following information shall be provided:

  • A Nomination Statement, succinctly summarising the accomplishments and background of the individual, written by the nominator (maximum 2 pages);
  • A CV of the candidate (maximum 4 pages);
  • Names and contact details of at least two other possible individuals, in addition to the nominator, that may provide additional support to the nomination.
